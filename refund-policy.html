3. Setup Fee Policy

⚠️ Important: Setup Fee is Non-Refundable

The $24.99 setup fee is non-refundable once your account has been activated and you have received your license key. This fee covers:

  • Lifetime access to basic ProfitPal features

  • License key generation and activation
  • Initial onboarding and support

3.1 Exceptions for Setup Fee Refunds

Setup fee refunds may be considered in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: If our service is completely inaccessible for more than 7 consecutive days due to technical problems on our end
  • Misrepresentation: If our service significantly differs from what was advertised
  • Payment Errors: Duplicate charges or billing errors

4. Monthly Subscription Refunds

4.1 Standard Refund Terms

  • 14-Day Refund Window: Request refunds within 14 days of billing
  • Pro-rated Refunds: Unused portion of the month will be refunded
  • Usage Consideration: Refunds may be denied if service was extensively used

4.2 Automatic Refund Scenarios

We will automatically process refunds for monthly subscriptions in these cases:

  • Service downtime exceeding 48 hours in a billing period
  • Duplicate billing due to technical errors
  • Billing after successful cancellation

5. Refund Request Process

5.1 How to Request a Refund

  1. Email us at denava.business@gmail.com
  2. Include the following information:
    • Your registered email address
    • Transaction ID or payment reference
    • Reason for refund request
    • Date of purchase
  3. Wait for review: We will review your request within 3-5 business days

5.2 Refund Processing Time

  • Approved refunds: Processed within 5-10 business days
  • Payment method: Refunded to original payment method via Paddle
  • Bank processing: May take additional 3-7 business days depending on your bank

6. Situations Where Refunds Are Not Available

Refunds will NOT be provided in the following situations:

  • Change of mind after using the service
  • Failure to cancel subscription before renewal
  • Violation of our Terms of Service
  • Account termination due to misuse
  • Technical issues on the user's end (internet, device, etc.)
  • Market losses or poor investment decisions based on our analysis
  • Requests made more than 30 days after purchase

7. Subscription Cancellation

7.1 How to Cancel

You can cancel your monthly subscription at any time by:

  • Emailing us at denava.business@gmail.com
  • The cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
  • You retain access to premium features until the billing period ends

7.2 What Happens After Cancellation
