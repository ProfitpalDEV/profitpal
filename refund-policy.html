3. Setup Fee Policy

⚠️ Important: Setup Fee is Non-Refundable

The $24.99 setup fee is non-refundable once your account has been activated and you have received your license key. This fee covers:

  • Account setup and configuration
  • License key generation and activation
  • Lifetime access to basic platform features
  • Initial onboarding and support

4. Monthly Subscription Policy

✅ Monthly Subscription Refunds

Monthly subscription fees of $4.99 may be refunded under the following conditions:

  • 14-day window: Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of billing
  • Pro-rated refunds: Based on unused service time in the billing period
  • Service issues: Technical problems preventing access for more than 72 hours
  • Billing errors: Duplicate charges or billing after cancellation

5. Refund Request Process

5.1 How to Request a Refund

  1. Send an email to denava.business@gmail.com
  2. Include your account email and transaction ID
  3. Specify the reason for the refund request
  4. Provide supporting documentation if applicable

5.2 Processing Timeline

  • Review Period: 5-7 business days
  • Processing Time: 3-10 business days after approval
  • Refund Method: Original payment method via Paddle

All refund requests are reviewed manually and processed at our discretion based on the specific circumstances.

6. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Setup fees after account activation and license key delivery
  • Monthly fees for services already accessed and used
  • Refund requests submitted more than 30 days after billing
  • Account suspension due to terms of service violations
  • Change of mind after successful service delivery and usage
  • Failure to cancel subscription before renewal

7. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your monthly subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting support. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. Your lifetime access (from the setup fee) remains valid after subscription cancellation.

8. Service Downtime Refunds

We will review and consider refunds for monthly subscriptions in these cases:

  • Service downtime exceeding 72 hours in a billing period (with documentation)
  • Duplicate billing due to technical errors (verified through payment records)
  • Billing after successful cancellation (with proof of cancellation)

9. Payment Processor

All payments are processed through Paddle.com. Refunds will be processed through the same system and may be subject to Paddle's policies and processing times.
